Who We Are

We are a group of UC Berkeley students with a passion for technology and helping others. Majoring in fields like computer science, statistics, business, and pre-med, we have diverse interests and a wide range of expertise. Our interests include finding creative solutions to challenging problems and providing technological innovation to campus organizations.

What We Do

We aim to build apps and services for these groups to help make their processes more efficient, and we provide support to help groups operate effectively and resolve issues by providing resources and tailored suggestions.
